Justin Timberlake business's

AfterMaster

Justin is the co-founder of Aftermaster. AfterMaster is a revolutionary breakthrough in audio processing that will change how we listen to audio forever. The company is based in. West Hollywood, California, United States

Bai

Timberlake is part the Bai team as an investor and its first chief flavor officer. Bai Brands is a beverage company founded in 2009 in Princeton, New Jersey.[1] The company offers a line of low-calorie soft drinks (including sodas, bottled water, iced tea, and non-carbonated fruit-flavored drinks) sweetened with erythritol and rebaudioside A (stevia leaf extract), ascorbic acid, and extract from coffea fruit harvested in Indonesia; He also is the face of the brand having signed a contract for a $100 million marketing campaign.

Checkout the Justin Timberlake Foundation
